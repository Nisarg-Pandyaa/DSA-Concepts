  - To Traverse AntiClockwise : `(i-1+n)%n` </br> </br>
  - Circular Distance, 
    - Direct Distance : `0`,...`LeftIndex` `->` `RigthIndex`,...`(n-1)` = `|RightIndex - LeftIndex|`
    - Circular Distance : `<-` `0` `<-` `LeftIndex`,....,`RightIndex` `<-` `(n-1)` `<-` = `n - |RightIndex - LeftIndex|`
